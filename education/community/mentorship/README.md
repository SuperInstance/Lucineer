# MLS Mentorship Program

## Overview

Pair experienced chip designers with learners for 1:1 guidance through the MLS certification path. Every student deserves access to an expert who's built real hardware.

## Program Structure

### Mentee Track
- **Duration:** 12 weeks (aligned with Chip Design 101)
- **Commitment:** 2 hours/week (1hr meeting + 1hr prep)
- **Cost:** Free for open-source contributors, $49/month otherwise
- **Matching:** Based on goals, timezone, and experience level

### Mentor Track
- **Commitment:** 2-4 hours/week (1-2 mentees)
- **Requirements:** MLS Professional or Fellow certification
- **Benefits:** CPE credits toward renewal, recognition, conference passes
- **Training:** 2-hour mentor orientation + quarterly calibration

## How It Works

### Week 1-4: Foundations
- Mentor reviews mentee's Lab 1-2 work
- Weekly 1:1 video calls
- Mentor shares their chip design war stories
- Goal setting and study plan

### Week 5-8: Deep Dive
- Mentor guides FPGA bringup (Lab 3)
- Code reviews on RTL designs
- Debug sessions on failing testbenches
- Career guidance (academia vs industry)

### Week 9-12: Capstone Support
- Mentor advises on capstone project selection
- Reviews architecture decisions
- Practice exam prep for MLS Associate
- Portfolio review and feedback

## For Mentors

### Getting Started
1. Apply at the mentorship portal
2. Complete 2-hour orientation
3. Get matched with 1-2 mentees
4. Receive mentor handbook and templates

### Meeting Templates
- **First meeting:** Introductions, goals, assessment of current level
- **Weekly check-in:** Review homework, discuss blockers, plan next week
- **Mid-point review:** Progress assessment, adjust study plan
- **Final review:** Capstone feedback, certification readiness, next steps

## Matching Criteria

| Factor | Weight |
|--------|--------|
| Timezone overlap (min 4 hrs) | Required |
| Language compatibility | Required |
| Technical area alignment | 40% |
| Industry experience match | 30% |
| Personality/style fit | 30% |

## Success Metrics

- Mentee passes MLS Associate: 85% target
- Mentee satisfaction: > 4.5/5
- Mentor satisfaction: > 4.0/5
- Mentee completes all 5 labs: 70% target
- Mentee contributes to open-source: 50% target
