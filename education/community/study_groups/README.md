# MLS Study Groups

## Overview

Self-organizing study groups for learners working through MLS courses and certification prep. Study together, debug together, succeed together.

## Group Types

### Course Cohorts
- **Size:** 5-8 students
- **Duration:** 12 weeks (Chip Design 101 schedule)
- **Meeting:** 2x/week, 90 minutes
- **Format:** Rotate facilitator, work through labs together

### Exam Prep
- **Size:** 3-5 students
- **Duration:** 4 weeks before exam date
- **Meeting:** 3x/week, 60 minutes
- **Format:** Practice questions, concept review, mock exams

### Research Reading Groups
- **Size:** 4-6 participants
- **Duration:** Ongoing
- **Meeting:** 1x/week, 60 minutes
- **Format:** Present and discuss one paper per session

## Weekly Format (Course Cohort)

### Session 1: Lab Work (90 min)
- **0-15 min:** Quick recap of week's content
- **15-75 min:** Work through lab exercises together
- **75-90 min:** Share solutions, discuss alternatives

### Session 2: Discussion (90 min)
- **0-15 min:** Each member shares what they learned
- **15-45 min:** Deep dive on most challenging topic
- **45-75 min:** Peer code review (swap RTL designs)
- **75-90 min:** Plan next week

## Starting a Study Group

1. Post in the community forum with your timezone and target course
2. Wait for 4+ members to join
3. Set up recurring calendar invite
4. Use the study group template repo for shared notes
5. Register your group for tracking and mentor assignment

## Tools

- **Video:** Jitsi Meet (self-hosted, no account required)
- **Chat:** Matrix/Element channel per group
- **Code:** Shared GitHub Codespace per group
- **Notes:** HackMD collaborative documents
- **Board:** Excalidraw for architecture whiteboarding
