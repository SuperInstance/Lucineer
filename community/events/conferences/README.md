# MLSConf — Annual Mask-Lock Standard Conference

## Overview

MLSConf is the annual gathering of the mask-lock hardware community. Two days of talks, workshops, demos, and networking.

## Format

### Day 1: Technical Program
- **Morning keynotes** (2 talks, 45 min each)
  - State of MLS ecosystem
  - Industry adoption keynote
- **Technical sessions** (4 parallel tracks, 20 min talks)
  - Track A: Hardware Design
  - Track B: Compiler & Tools
  - Track C: Privacy & Security
  - Track D: Applications & Deployments
- **Poster session** (90 min, with refreshments)
- **Evening reception**

### Day 2: Hands-On
- **Morning workshops** (3 hours, choose one)
  - Workshop 1: Build Your First Mask-Lock Chip (beginner)
  - Workshop 2: CLAWC Compiler Deep Dive (intermediate)
  - Workshop 3: Multi-Chip Cascade Design (advanced)
- **Afternoon unconference** (community-proposed topics)
- **MLS Fellow inductions**
- **Chipathon kickoff** (runs through weekend)

## Call for Papers

### Topics of Interest
- Novel mask-lock architectures
- Ternary quantization techniques
- FPGA prototyping results
- ASIC tape-out experience reports
- Privacy cascade implementations
- Compiler optimizations for ternary
- Application case studies
- Formal verification of MLS designs
- Side-channel analysis and countermeasures
- Education and workforce development

### Submission Types
| Type | Length | Review |
|------|--------|--------|
| Full paper | 6-8 pages | Double-blind |
| Short paper | 3-4 pages | Double-blind |
| Demo | 2-page abstract | Single-blind |
| Poster | 1-page abstract | Single-blind |
| Workshop proposal | 2-page description | Invited |

### Timeline (Annual)
- **January:** Call for papers opens
- **April:** Submission deadline
- **June:** Acceptance notification
- **August:** Camera-ready deadline
- **October:** Conference

## Venue

Rotates annually between regions:
- Americas (odd years)
- Europe/Africa (even years)
- Asia-Pacific (every third year starting 2029)
